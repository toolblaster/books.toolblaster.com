About the Author

VR

Vikas Rana

Vikas Rana writes clear, practical guides to help readers build real-life skills and grow with confidence. His no-nonsense approach turns everyday challenges into simple steps for personal and professional success.

Always curious, Vikas is passionate about exploring new topics and sharing fresh insights to empower readers on their journey.

A Note from Vikas:

"I believe the most important lessons aren't always taught in a classroom. My mission is to create simple, engaging guides that empower children with the confidence to navigate life's challenges and seize its opportunities. I hope these books become a trusted friend on your journey."
